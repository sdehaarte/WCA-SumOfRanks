Sum of Ranks

About

The WCA website already features Sum of Ranks single and average as separate categories. However, single is influenced by
lucky scrambles and average doesn't include multi-blind. To fix this, we combined both into one metric! "Sum of Ranks" uses
your average world rank for all sighted events, and your single rank for the blinds! While 3bld, 4bld and 5bld have averages,
we used singles in the calculation since many good bigbld competitors don't have averages, and competitions use bo3 for placement.
